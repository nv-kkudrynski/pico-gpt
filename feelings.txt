Love is a profound and passionate affection towards someone.
Hate is an intense dislike or aversion towards a person or thing.
Joy is a feeling of great pleasure and happiness.
Sadness is a state of feeling sorrowful or unhappy.
Anger is a strong feeling of annoyance, displeasure, or hostility.
Fear is an unpleasant emotion caused by the belief that someone or something is dangerous, likely to cause pain, or a threat.
Surprise is a sudden and unexpected event or piece of information.
Disgust is a strong feeling of disapproval and revulsion.
Trust is firm belief in the reliability, truth, ability, or strength of someone or something.
Anticipation is a feeling of excitement about something that is going to happen.
